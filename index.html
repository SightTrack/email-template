What is SightTrack?

SightTrack is a citizen science platform that engages volunteers in wildlife conservation by documenting and tracking wildlife sightings. Participants contribute valuable data for research and conservation efforts while earning volunteer hours for their academic and professional development. Our platform validates and calculates volunteer hours based on the time, effort, and scientific value of each contribution.
